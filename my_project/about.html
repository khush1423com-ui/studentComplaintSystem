About Student Complaint System

About Our Project

The Student Complaint System is designed to provide students with an easy and effective way to submit their problems and concerns directly to the college administration. Our goal is to create a transparent and efficient communication system between students and management.

Our Motivation

Many students face issues in college but hesitate to speak up due to lack of proper platforms. This system encourages students to share their concerns without fear and ensures that their voices are heard.

Guidelines for Students

  • Submit genuine and clear complaints only.
  • Provide proper details for faster resolution.
  • Avoid using inappropriate language.
  • Do not submit duplicate complaints.
  • Use the system responsibly.

Our Vision

To build a better and more student-friendly environment where every problem is addressed and every student feels valued.
